Our Promise

Here are our ideas to improve the CMU experience



Fiscal Transformation

Collaboration

Mental Health & Wellness

Diversity

Fiscal Transformation

  • Collaborate with Student Government to make finances more transparent

  • Digitize financial processes for organizations

  • Save organizations time with the financial workflow and allow them to focus more on their initiatives

Collaboration

  • Spearhead the One CMU initiative - focus on reducing the department specific mindset and replacing it with a collaborative CMU spirit

  • Advocate for collaboration across different academic departments and majors. E.g. combine redundant department events into one larger event

  • Hold mixers between students of all majors

Mental Health and Wellness

  • Support the administration to revamp mental health policies to be more supportive of students

  • Collaborate with Division Affairs to ensure greater utilization of CAPS

  • Work with SLICE, Senate, GSA, and CMU Task Force to promote a healthy culture at CMU

Diversity

  • Strive to ensure that the Carnegie Mellon community is one that is welcoming to students of all backgrounds regardless of race, gender, gender identification, sexual orientation, etc.

  • Work with the university to go beyond just aiding diverse candidates to get their foot in the door
