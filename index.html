Fiscal Transformation


  • Work with CMU administration to transform financial processes for organizations to be online

    • Will save organizations lots of time with the financial workflow and allow them to focus more on their initiatives

    • Will eliminate grunt work from SLICE's workflow and allow them to better serve students and organizations


  • Continue making progress on Fiscal transparency

    • Collaborate with Student Government legislative bodies to make their finances more transparent

Collaboration


  • Advocate for collaboration across different academic departments and majors

    • Combine redundant department events into one larger event


  • Hold mixers between students of all majors


  • Collaborate with CMU Administration on One CMU initiative - focus on reducing the department specific mindset and replacing it with a collaborative CMU spirit

Mental Health and Wellness


  • Work with administration to revamp mental health policies to be more supportive of students


  • Collaborate with Division Affairs to ensure the transformation of CAPS promotes the utilization of CAPS

    • Streamline the process of making an appointment

    • Hire more internal CMU psychiatrists


  • Work with SLICE, Senate, GSA, and CMU Task Force to promote a healthy culture at CMU

Diversity


  • Works towards ensuring Carnegie Mellon Community is one that is welcoming to students of all backgrounds regardless of race, gender, gender identification, sexual orientation, etc.


  • Work with University to go beyond just aiding diverse candidates get their foot in the door

    • Create a first general faculty student mentor network

    • Provide students from underserved communities with the resources needed to help them succeed at CMU

    • Work with CMU Administration to hold events and trainings that provide students with the information and insights they need to succeed in their careers beyond CMU
